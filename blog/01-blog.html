Why Write Blogs?



Why Blogs are useful

Last week, I was searching for useful information and any content on how a boat charter process would look like. Very soon I realized, that the standard, big websites only contained very shallow knowledge about this topic. They were all lacking an important thing to me: Real-life examples. And this is, where a great (German) blog by Emanuel Grand helped me out, describing his experience on his first charter, including everything I searched for.

This personal experience is something, big websites are lacking. And this doesn't only go for sailing. Nearly every topic you can think of can be explored on a much more personal level. Wether it is useful apps, how to improve productivity, sports, life hacks and much more, all of those topics can be deeply explored in a blog post.

Why you should start a blog

The answer to the question, why you should start your own blog, is quiet simple: Self Improvement.

How to get started

Now although only one thing is left, it is also the hardest step: getting started. But rest assured, starting your own blog doesn't require much at all. A pen & paper, smartphone, or laptop is all you need.
